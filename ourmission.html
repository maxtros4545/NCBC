OUR MISSION

Our Mission

We are passionate about helping believers connect with God through Scripture and testimonies. We focus on teaching and shedding light on spiritual and prophetic meanings in the Word that can be applied to believers’ lives.

Mission Section

Mission: What are we doing?

“Know Christ. Love the Church. Serve the City.”

Know Christ

Christ is at the centre of all we do, and we long to know Him and the power of his resurrection (Philippians 3:10).

Love The Church

Jesus said his disciples would be known because of their love for one another (John 13:35).

Serve The City

We strive to shine the light of Christ in Brisbane and beyond through our words, deeds, and work (Matthew 5:16).

Go therefore and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, teaching them to observe all that I have commanded you.
