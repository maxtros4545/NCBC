Special Characteristics

Special Characteristics

These are the special characteristics which flavour the lesson material and ministry approach of the Online Bible College:

The Centrality of the Gospel

We believe that the Gospel of the Lord Jesus Christ must be central to all church teaching and activity. The Gospel is a "whole Gospel for the whole Christian life."

The Gifts of the Spirit

We believe in the empowering of the Holy Spirit for service and the present-day operation of the gifts of the Spirit, as described in 1 Corinthians 12:7-11.

The Ministry of Every Believer

We believe that every believer, regardless of race, gender, or educational status, is called by God to participate in His glorious purpose.

The Unity of the Body of Christ

We believe in the indivisible unity of the Body of Christ, established solely upon the foundation of the Gospel of Christ, as described in John 17:20-23 and Ephesians 4:13.

NORTH CALOOCAN BIBLE COLLEGE

North Caloocan Bible College (NCBC) is a faith-based institution committed to providing quality biblical education and ministerial training. Located in North Caloocan, Philippines, NCBC equips students with theological knowledge, leadership skills, and a strong spiritual foundation. The college offers various programs designed to prepare individuals for pastoral ministry, missionary work, and church leadership. At NCBC, students receive in-depth instruction in biblical studies, theology, and Christian discipleship. The curriculum integrates academic learning with practical ministry experience, ensuring that graduates are well-prepared to serve in their respective callings. The faculty consists of dedicated pastors and scholars who guide students in deepening their faith and understanding of God‚Äôs Word. Beyond academics, NCBC fosters a Christ-centered community where students can grow spiritually through worship, prayer, and fellowship. The institution emphasizes character development, servant leadership, and a heart for evangelism. Through outreach programs and hands-on ministry opportunities, students engage with local churches and communities, putting their faith into action. North Caloocan Bible College remains committed to training the next generation of Christian leaders, empowering them to spread the gospel and make a meaningful impact both locally and globally. Its mission is to glorify God by raising up faithful servants dedicated to His work.
