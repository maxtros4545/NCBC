ACTIVITIES

Here Are Our Activities

Church Development

We focus on fostering a welcoming and united church community where members find spiritual nourishment and a sense of belonging.

Charity & Donation

We are committed to acts of charity and community support, extending God’s love to those in need.

Bible Learning

Our teachings empower spiritual growth and transformation through active engagement with Scripture.

Special Programs

We host a variety of special programs throughout the year to enrich the spiritual life of our congregation.

Parent Education

Our Parent Education program equips parents with knowledge and skills to raise children with strong moral values.

Evangelism and Soul Winning

We passionately share the message of Christ’s love and salvation through outreach activities.
