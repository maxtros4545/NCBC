Our Values

JCSIA

WORSHIP

Our foundation is built on worship. We believe in the power of lifting our voices and hearts to God, celebrating His goodness, and encountering His presence.

J.O.Y.

We seek to live out the transformative power of Jesus, Others, You. By prioritizing Jesus above all else, and then caring for others, we empower ourselves to better serve our community and grow in our faith walk.

BEING SENT

Being sent means actively engaging with our local and global communities, meeting people where they are, and sharing the love and hope of Jesus through our words and actions.
