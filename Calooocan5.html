At North Caloocan Bible College, we are dedicated to equipping students with biblical knowledge, spiritual growth, and leadership skills. Our mission is to develop strong, faith-driven individuals who will serve God and their communities with wisdom and compassion. Indiana Bible College Minors

Minors

Biblical Studies

The Biblical Studies minor at North Caloocan Bible College focuses on training Pastor ministry with the necessary tools for effective ministry, emphasizing deep theological understanding, practical leadership skills, and a commitment to biblical truth. This program prepares students to lead congregations with integrity, wisdom, and a heart for service.

Missiology

The Missiology minor is designed to provide training for missionaries and others who serve or plan to serve in intercultural ministry and church multiplication positions.

Christian Leadership

The Christian Leadership minor at NCBC Bible College trains students to be Biblically centered, spiritually sensitive, and technically capable, equipping them to effectively lead churches and ministries into future growth, while fostering a deep understanding of leadership principles grounded in faith and service.

Join Us for Bible School Service This Weekend

We’re a vibrant, multiethnic, multigenerational community, united in our mission to serve God and spread His love. We believe that the Church isn’t just a building – it’s a people, a family, bound together by faith, love, and purpose. At the heart of our mission is the belief that we are called to love God completely, to understand and embrace His love for us, and to live out that love in our relationships with ourselves and with others. Our community is made up of people from all walks of life, different cultures, and varying backgrounds. We value the richness that diversity brings, knowing that it reflects the beauty of God's creation. We are committed to creating a welcoming environment where everyone can experience God's love and grow in their faith journey, no matter where they are in life. This weekend, we invite you to join us as we come together to worship, learn, and connect as a family. Whether you're new to the faith or have been walking with God for years, there’s a place for you here. We believe that each person is unique and valuable, and we’re excited to see how God will move in your life. Come, be part of something bigger than yourself. Let’s love God, love ourselves, and love our neighbors, and let’s do it together. We hope to see you this weekend!

Campus Page

Campus

JCSIA and NCBC Church campuses serve as vibrant centers for spiritual growth, education, and community engagement. JCSIA fosters a dynamic learning environment, equipping students with academic excellence and leadership skills. Meanwhile, NCBC Church provides a place of worship, fellowship, and outreach programs that enrich lives through faith and service. Both institutions work together to create a holistic experience, combining knowledge with spiritual nourishment. With a commitment to uplifting individuals and communities, they offer various programs, events, and initiatives that inspire growth, unity, and purpose. Their shared mission is to empower people to lead with wisdom, integrity, and compassion.
