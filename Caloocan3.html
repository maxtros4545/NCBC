Our Calling to Share the Word

Who We Are

In these times when people need the Word of God more than ever, the Philippine Bible Society is on a mission to bring the Word of God to those who seek it and those whom it seeks, in order to bring hope, light, and transformation that can only come from His Word.

The Philippine Bible Society (PBS) is a non-profit, non-stock, interconfessional organization that exists to provide people with Scriptures in the language they understand, in formats they prefer, and at prices they can afford.

PBS is a member of the United Bible Societies, a worldwide network of Bible Societies operating in over 240 countries and territories to make sure that everyone who wants to can access and engage with the Bible.
