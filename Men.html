THE FOUNDATION

Men’s ministry is a group or program within a church that is specifically designed to minister to the needs of men. The ministry is focused on helping men grow in their faith, develop healthy relationships with other men, and become better leaders in their homes, churches, and communities.

Vision & Mission

OUR VISION

A school that produces godly leaders for the country and other parts of the world: spiritual leaders who accurately handle the Word of truth, Christlike in character, relevant and responsive to the needs of the time in fulfilling the Great Commission.

MISSION

The school aims to provide opportunities for excellent training and development of men and women of God, Christian leaders who will excel and have the spiritual cutting edge in various areas of ministry such as pastorate, missions and evangelism, Bible teaching, Christian Education, and Church Music.

THE PURPOSE OF MEN’S MINISTRY

The purpose of the Men’s Ministry is to empower men to men and to accept the responsibility to lead at home as well as in the church. This is the focus of the Men’s Ministry – to study and know the Word of God and to accept and apply the biblical leadership mandate in the lives of men.

Our Goal

The goal of the Men’s Ministry is to bring men into a love relationship with God. This relationship, once it is strengthened, will drive everything that a man does. It can only be done through education about God, who he is and what he stands for which develops a standard for men.

OUR PROGRAM

Our men's ministry program can include a variety of activities and events that help men grow spiritually, socialize, and serve the community.

  • Twice a month gatherings and Bible studies.
  • Provide opportunities to support each other and discuss faith.
  • Retreats and conferences.
  • Promote deeper participation and introspection.
  • Service projects.
  • Allow men to contribute to the community and demonstrate their religion through deeds.
  • Mentoring - more experienced members can serve as mentors to newer members.
  • Small groups - Men can come together weekly in small accountability and prayer groups.
