    College Programs

    College Programs

    The Bachelor of Arts in Theology provides a comprehensive study of Christian doctrines, biblical interpretation, and church history. Students develop critical thinking skills in theology, ethics, and pastoral care, preparing for ministry, teaching, or further theological studies in various religious and academic settings.
    The Bachelor of Arts in Christian Education equips students with biblical knowledge and pedagogical skills to teach and disciple others effectively. This program prepares graduates for roles in church education, youth ministries, and Christian schools, emphasizing curriculum development, leadership, and faith formation.
    The Bachelor of Theology in Biblical Studies focuses on an in-depth analysis of Scripture, theological principles, and biblical languages. Students gain exegetical and hermeneutical skills, preparing them for pastoral ministry, teaching, or advanced theological research in biblical interpretation and doctrine.
    The Bachelor of Pastoral Leadership trains individuals for effective church leadership, pastoral care, and discipleship. The curriculum includes biblical studies, leadership principles, preaching, and church administration, equipping students to serve as pastors, ministry leaders, or mentors in Christian communities..
    The Bachelor of Christian Counselling integrates psychology and biblical principles to prepare students for faith-based counselling roles. The program covers pastoral care, crisis intervention, and therapeutic techniques, equipping graduates to support individuals and families in spiritual and emotional healing.
    Certificate programs offer foundational theological training for those seeking ministry skills without committing to a full degree. These programs cover biblical studies, discipleship, and leadership, equipping students with practical knowledge for local church service, missionary work, or personal spiritual growth.
    The Master of Divinity in Biblical Studies provides advanced theological education in Scripture, systematic theology, and pastoral ministry. Designed for aspiring pastors and scholars, it equips students with exegetical, leadership, and preaching skills essential for church ministry and academic research.
    The Master in Biblical Counselling deepens students' understanding of faith-based counselling by integrating theological and psychological principles. It prepares individuals for advanced counselling roles in churches, clinics, and Christian organizations, emphasizing pastoral care and biblical wisdom in addressing life challenges.
    The Master in Pastoral Leadership strengthens leadership skills for pastors and ministry leaders. This program covers church administration, pastoral theology, and discipleship strategies, equipping students to lead congregations effectively while fostering spiritual growth and community engagement.
    The Master in Christian Education focuses on advanced biblical teaching methodologies, curriculum design, and faith-based pedagogy. It prepares educators and ministry leaders to develop effective Christian education programs in churches, schools, and seminaries, emphasizing discipleship and spiritual formation.

    North Caloocan Bible College Today

    By God's grace, North Caloocan Bible College remains steadfast in upholding high academic standards and holistic training for future pastors, missionaries, Christian educators, music ministers, and leaders since its founding.

    The college continues to fulfill its mission through undergraduate and graduate education, professional development, and opportunities for theological, technical, and practical research.

    At its core, North Caloocan Bible College exists for two purposes: to glorify God and to equip His servants in building up the body of Christ, ensuring they are prepared for impactful ministry and service.

    What are our Core Values

    CORE VALUES ARE THE ETHICAL VALUES THAT ARE IMPORTANT TO US AND GUIDE US IN OUR MINISTRY. THEY ARE THE VALUES THAT PRESCRIBE THE ATTITUDE AND CHARACTER OF AN ORGANIZATION.

    1 BIBLICALLY focused training so that church leaders become more effective in using the Word of God in their personal lives and ministry (2 Tim 3:16-17)
    2 AFFORDABLE training so that all Christ followers can access excellent ministry training and not just a few who can afford it
    3 CULTURALLY relevant in our approach, strategy, and presentation (Acts 17:22-33)
