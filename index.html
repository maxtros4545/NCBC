Charis Bible College Message

God has more for you

In a world full of lies and distractions, many Christians struggle to find, follow, and fulfill their God-given purpose. Confusion from false religion and past hardships prevents us from knowing God and who He created us to be. Are you ready for a change in your life?

WE WANT TO HELP YOU

At North Calooca Bible College we guide you into discovering your purpose by immersing you in the Word of God. Receive transformational knowledge as you cultivate a vibrant, personal relationship with Jesus. It is the knowledge and experience of His love that will change you and the world around you.

Vision

The Vision of NCBC is to educate the next generation in God's word for them to be anexcellent teachers, Missionaries, Pastors, and leaders of Christ.

Mission

The Mission of NCBC is to follow the Great Commission of the Lord in Matthew 28:18-20. To train leaders for the Expansion of God's kingdom

Values

The Core Values of NCBC is to attain Christ likeness by studying God's word for the glory of God and for the Salvation of Humanity

Ministry Features
1. BIBLICAL TRAINING

Our first priority is to train our students to properly handle the Word of God.

2. LEADERSHIP SKILLS

We believe leadership is vitally important to ministry and we inject biblical leadership principles into all of our training.

3. ENGLISH PROFICIENCY

English is the global language of ministry and our students are equipped with a knowledge of English to propel them into effective global ministry.
