Explore Program and Chapel
Explore Program

The Explore Program in our Bible college blends spiritual growth with academic discovery. Students deepen their faith, develop leadership skills, and explore their God-given purpose. Through ministry training, discipleship, and college-level courses, they are equipped to impact communities and serve boldly in their calling for Christ.

As part of our core curriculum, our Explore Program requires every student to participate in hands-on ministry, leadership development, and biblical studies. This experience empowers students to grow spiritually, discover their calling, and apply God’s Word in real-life settings, preparing them for impactful service in ministry and beyond.

Chapel

Our students gather for chapel twice a week to spend time fostering their relationships with Jesus and reorienting themselves toward Him. The service format varies widely throughout the year—sometimes there is teaching and worship, other times the entire service is dedicated to prayer—but no matter the format, deepening our relationship with Christ is always the goal.

Confessional

The Confessional aspect of our program encourages students to embrace transparency, accountability, and spiritual growth. Through guided reflection, prayer, and mentoring, students confess struggles and victories, fostering healing and deeper intimacy with God. It’s a safe space to grow in holiness and build authentic Christian community. .

Core Curriculum

Our Core Curriculum lays a strong foundation in biblical studies, theology, and spiritual formation. It equips students with essential knowledge of God’s Word, leadership principles, and ministry skills. Designed to shape character and calling, it prepares students for lifelong service in the church, missions, and Christian leadership.

Ministry and Marketplace

The Ministry and Marketplace program integrates faith with practical skills, preparing students to serve both in church settings and the professional world. It equips them to be effective leaders, entrepreneurs, and influencers, bringing biblical values into every sphere of life, from business to social impact and community development.

Faculty

Our faculty consists of experienced educators, pastors, and ministry leaders committed to shaping the next generation of Christian leaders. With a passion for teaching and mentorship, they provide academic excellence, biblical wisdom, and real-world ministry experience, guiding students to fulfill their God-given calling and make a lasting impact.
