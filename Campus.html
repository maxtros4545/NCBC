OCU Marketplace Ministers

Connecting Campus Ministry with the Church: Building a Strong Foundation for Faith

Connecting campus ministry with the church creates a solid foundation for spiritual growth. By fostering relationships between students and church communities, both can support one another in faith. Campus ministries help students grow spiritually, while the church provides guidance and accountability, ensuring lifelong discipleship and a lasting impact for Christ.

The Importance of Fellowship: Growing Together in Christ –

Fellowship is vital for spiritual growth, as it strengthens relationships with God and fellow believers. In community, we encourage one another, share our burdens, and grow in faith together. Through mutual support and accountability, fellowship fosters a deeper understanding of Christ’s love, making us stronger in our walk with Him.

The Power of Prayer in College Life"

Prayer is a powerful tool in college life, offering comfort, guidance, and strength during stressful times. It connects us with God, providing clarity and peace in the midst of academic challenges. Through prayer, we seek direction, find inner peace, and experience God's presence in every aspect of our journey.

Called to Ministry?

Being called to ministry is a divine invitation to serve others and share God's love. It requires faith, obedience, and a heart willing to serve. Whether through preaching, teaching, or supporting others, ministry is about fulfilling God’s purpose and making a difference in the lives of those around us.

Our Name

Our Campus Ministry is Light of Faith Campus Ministry We are a community of students united in faith, committed to growing spiritually, and spreading God's love. Our name reflects our mission to be a guiding light, leading others to Christ through worship, service, and discipleship..

Our Dream

Our dream is to build a faith "driven student community where everyone encounters Christ’s love. We envision a campus filled with transformed lives, strong in faith, and committed to serving others. We aspire to be a beacon of hope, where students grow spiritually and share God's love with the world.

Our Strategy

We cultivate faith through weekly Bible studies,Sunday services. We encourage, service through outreach programs, mentoring, and discipleship "We foster unity by organizing retreats, fellowship gatherings, and mission trips. Our strategy is to equip students to live out their faith boldly and impact their campus positively."

Our Mission

Our mission is to lead students to Christ strengthen their faith, and empower them to share the Gospel. Through prayer, worship nd service, we seek to create a Christ-centered community that inspires spiritual growth, encourages discipleship, and transforms lives, equipping students to be God’s light on campus and beyond.
