Vision & Mission

Pagpapalakas ng Pag-aasawa

Ang kasal ay itinalaga ng Diyos (Genesis 2:24, Efeso 5:31), at ang ministeryo para sa mag-asawa ay tumutulong sa kanila na itugma ang kanilang relasyon sa mga prinsipyong nakabatay sa Biblia.

Pagbuo ng Matatag na Pamilya

Ang matatag na kasal ang pundasyon ng malusog na pamilya, na siyang bumubuo ng malalakas na simbahan at komunidad. Ang ministeryo para sa mag-asawa ay nagbibigay ng kasanayan upang maging mas mabuting magulang at huwaran para sa kanilang mga anak, na nagdudulot ng biyaya sa susunod na henerasyon.

Pagbibigay ng Suporta

Ang ministeryo sa simbahan ay lumilikha ng komunidad kung saan maaaring magbahagi ng karanasan, humingi ng payo, at ipanalangin ang isa’t isa. Tinutulungan nitong maunawaan ng mga mag-asawa na hindi sila nag-iisa sa kanilang mga pagsubok at nagbibigay ito ng ligtas na lugar para sa paggaling at paglago.

Pag-iwas sa Krisis sa Pag-aasawa

Regular na pagtuturo at pagpapayo sa pamamagitan ng mga workshop, retreat, at seminar ay nakatutulong upang matukoy at masolusyonan ang mga problema bago ito lumala. Natututo ang mga mag-asawa ng tamang pakikipagkasundo, wastong pamamahala ng pananalapi, at pagiging espiritwal na lider, na nakababawas sa posibilidad ng paghihiwalay.

Pagpapalago sa Pananampalataya

Hinihikayat ng ministeryo ang mga mag-asawa na magkasamang manalangin, mag-aral ng Biblia, at maglingkod, na nagdadala sa mas malalim na relasyon nila sa Diyos at sa isa’t isa. Tinutulungan nito ang mga mag-asawa na lumago bilang mga alagad ni Cristo, binibigyang-diin ang kanilang papel bilang katuwang sa pananampalataya.

Paglinang ng Pag-iisip na Makalangit

Sa pamamagitan ng pagtuturo sa mga mag-asawa na ang kanilang kasal ay isang ministeryo rin, sila’y pinalalakas na maglingkod nang magkasama, mag-mentor sa mga nakababatang mag-asawa, at tumulong sa misyon ng simbahan.

Pagtugon sa Panlipunang Pagsubok

Sa mundong madalas na minamaliit o binabago ang kahulugan ng kasal, pinalalakas ng ministeryo ang pananaw na nakabatay sa Biblia at tinutulungan ang mga mag-asawa na labanan ang mga presyur mula sa lipunan.

Konklusyon

Ang ministeryo para sa mag-asawa ay mahalaga para sa espiritwal at relasyunal na kapakanan ng parehong mag-asawa at ng komunidad ng simbahan. Sa pagpapahalaga sa ministeryong ito, ang simbahan ay hindi lamang namumuhunan sa mga indibidwal na relasyon kundi nagpapalakas din sa mas malawak na katawan ni Cristo.

Vision

To see marriages transformed by the Gospel, where couples thrive in love, mutual respect, and purpose, becoming a testimony of God’s design for marriage and an influence for His Kingdom within their families, church, and community

Mission

To glorify God by equipping couples to build strong, Christ-centered marriages that reflect the love of Christ and His Church, fostering spiritual growth, unity, and lasting commitment..

Key Goals of a Couples Ministry

1.Provide biblical teaching on marriage principles.

2.Create opportunities for fellowship, mentorship, and accountability among couples

3.Offer counseling, prayer support, and resources for couples in need

4.Equip couples to serve and disciple others in their community.

5.Celebrate and strengthen marriages through retreats, workshops, and special events.

Biblical Foundations

1.Reflecting Christ and the Church (Eph. 5:25-33)

2.Unity and Partnership (Gen 2:24)

3.Encouragement and Support (Ecc 4:9-12)

4.Generational Impact (Deut 6:6-7)

Mission & Vision

United in Faith: Building the Church of the Home with Husband and Wife

Our mission is to glorify God by equipping couples to build strong, Christ-centered marriages that reflect the love of Christ and His Church. By fostering spiritual growth, unity, and lasting commitment, we seek to help couples deepen their relationship with God and each other. Through biblical teaching, prayer, and practical guidance, we aim to strengthen marriages, empowering couples to serve one another selflessly and reflect the character of Christ in their daily lives. These transformed marriages will be a powerful testimony of God’s grace, bringing glory to Him and impacting families, communities, and the world for His Kingdom.

Families Empowered by the Holy Spirit: Renewing the Earth

To witness marriages transformed by the Gospel, where couples flourish in love, mutual respect, and purpose, growing in faith and unity. These relationships will reflect God’s design for marriage, becoming powerful testimonies that impact their families, churches, and communities. As they nurture their bond, they will inspire others to embrace God's vision for marriage. Their example will serve as a lasting influence, demonstrating how His grace strengthens and sustains relationships. Through their commitment to each other and to God, these couples will play a vital role in advancing His Kingdom, leaving an enduring legacy of faith and love.

Couples Information

ENGAGED COUPLES

Congratulations! Preparing for marriage is one of the most important things you’ll ever do. While you’re making plans for that special day, don’t forget to focus on what matters most – your life together after the wedding.

At Evangel Church, we want to help you invest in your marriage long-term, and so we offer a premarital class called SYMBIS. SYMBIS, which stands for Saving Your Marriage Before It Starts, is the required premarital class for any couple looking to get married at Evangel or by an Evangel pastor.

SYMBIS classes are offered twice per year, once in the Spring and once in the Fall. To get started with SYMBIS, click the button below.

MARRIED COUPLES

Whether you’ve been married for 5 months, 5 years, or 5 decades, life transitions; hardships; children; and even just getting older can wear you down. Join other couples in a LifeGroup setting who want to keep the flame of love burning brightly and have the marriage God intends for you to have.

You may even want to consider starting up a Married Couples LifeGroup. Click below to join our interest list to start a group or be notified of upcoming groups.
