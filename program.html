Vision and Mission
Vision:

To become a primary learning destination for preparing Kingdom workers.

Mission:

RBC exists to prepare kingdom workers through:

  • Collegiate Biblical education.
  • Personal relationships formed in the context of an authentic community.
  • Experiential learning opportunities and skill development.
  • Spirit-led prayer, worship, and devotional Bible reading.
Student Graduation Outcome
Our students graduate with:
  • A deeper knowledge of God and themselves.
  • An informed understanding of their faith.
  • Practical life skills.
  • Transformative life experiences.
  • A transferable associate degree.
Quick Facts

Quick Facts:

Location:

Located in NCBC, our campus provides a transformative environment where students engage in rigorous biblical education, practical ministry, and spiritual development. Surrounded by a supportive community, students are equipped to grow in faith, leadership, and purpose, preparing them to impact the world with the message of Christ.

Cost:

The tuition fees for NCBC are designed to be affordable and accessible. Upon enrollment, a fee of Php 250.00 is required. For the trimester, covering 12 meetings, the tuition fee is Php 1,200.00, ensuring students receive quality biblical education at a reasonable cost for their academic journey. This structure allows students to focus on their spiritual and academic growth without financial strain. With a commitment to excellence, NCBC strives to make Christian education accessible to all, empowering students to pursue their calling with a strong foundation in faith and knowledge.

Student Life:

Student life at NCBC is vibrant, supportive, and centered on spiritual growth. Students engage in community activities, ministry opportunities, and leadership development. With a focus on building lasting friendships and cultivating faith, NCBC provides an environment where students are encouraged to grow academically, spiritually, and personally in Christ.

Affiliation:

NCBC is affiliated with a network of like-minded Christian organizations and ministries committed to equipping students for service in the church and the world. This affiliation strengthens the college’s mission, providing students with access to a broader community of faith, resources, and opportunities to serve globally.

Financial Aid:

NCBC offers financial aid options to help make education accessible. Through scholarships, grants, and payment plans, students receive support to ease the financial burden. The college is committed to ensuring that all students, regardless of their financial situation, have the opportunity to pursue their calling and educational goals.

Enrollment:

Enrollment at NCBC is simple and straightforward. Prospective students can apply online or in person, submitting required documents and paying the enrollment fee. Once accepted, students can register for classes, begin their academic journey, and become part of a vibrant Christian community committed to spiritual and academic growth.

Academic Programs

Our Programs

Designed to offer students a balance of academic preparation, spiritual growth and personal development

Associate Degree

Through our well-rounded two-year Associate Degree in Biblical Studies, students complete their general education classes while establishing an informed understanding of your faith.

2+2 Transfer Agreements

Rosedale graduates can transfer to one of our partner schools to finish their bachelor's degree. In some cases, this is able to be done in just two additional years.

Mentorship Groups

Our mentorship program provides both spiritual discipleship and practical life skills training to prepare students for life after college.

Transformative Learning

Educational opportunities like our cross-cultural learning program and the Akwachink Leadership School give students challenging, real-world experience.

Core Values - Rosedale Bible College

Bible College:

  • The Word of God. We are committed to the belief that the Word of God is powerful and life-giving through the ministry of the Holy Spirit (Hebrews 4:12; II Timothy 3:16–17).
  • The Gospel Message. We are committed to the truth of the Gospel message.
  • Evangelical Anabaptism. We are committed to a theological perspective of Evangelical Anabaptism because it reflects the teaching and revelation of Jesus Christ.
  • Partnerships. We are committed to partnerships with local congregations, working together to empower communities, support spiritual growth, develop leaders, and advance the mission of the church through the shared vision of NCBC and JCSIA
  • Academic Excellence. We are committed to academic excellence for the glory of God.
  • Community. We are committed to a nurturing, authentic Christian community.
  • Cultural Engagement. We are committed to equipping people to engage the broader culture with the Gospel.
