Our Mission

OUR VISSION

“OUR VISION IS TO SEE EVERY MEMBER OF JCSIA PROCLAIMING THE GOSPEL OF JESUS CHRIST IN EVERY PLACE” [“Now those who were scattered went about preaching the word.”[Acts 8:4]”

Acts 8:4 (KJV): "Therefore they that were scattered abroad went every where preaching the word." This verse highlights how the early Christians, despite persecution, spread the Gospel wherever they went. It aligns well with your vision of proclaiming Jesus Christ in every place! Let me know if you need help incorporating this into a project or design.

Find Identity & Purpose

FIND IDENTITY

One of Jesus' values throughout His ministry is the feeling of family that ran through his disciples, through those who He ministered to! Jesus was always inviting people into His Kingdom and to be a co-heir to that Kingdom. Even on the cross, Jesus was still welcoming people and one of his last acts was welcoming someone else into Heaven.

FIND PURPOSE

Each disciple Jesus called had a purpose within the ultimate goal of Jesus. Everyone played a part, even Judas. This is important in the Kingdom. Many people are walking around and have a desire in their hearts to serve, they just don’t know where. We want those who walk into this church to know their God-given purpose!
