WHAT TO EXPECT AT DREAM

We are so glad you’re considering visiting us here at Dream Church! You don’t have to believe to belong, come as you are! We are excited for what God has in store for you! Here is what you can expect!

PASTORAL

As soon as you pull onto our property you will be greeted by our Parking lot Experience team, who will assist you on finding the best place to park!

SUNDAY SERVICE

Enjoy a free cup of coffee as you walk in! We want you to feel at home, and what better way than with a warm cup of coffee and a friendly face!

WELCOME CENTER JCSIA

KIDS CHECK-IN

If you have kiddos from 0-5th grade we have Little Dreamers and Dream Kids they can attend! A Dream teamer will help you through the check-in process.

ENJOY SERVICE

We have an incredible worship experience followed by an impactful word planned for you. We have been praying and believing for God to do something new and impactful in your life, welcome to the family! We hope you enjoy your time here with us at Dream Church.

SCHEDULE A VISIT

If you are considering visiting Dream Church, we would love to accommodate you the best we can! If you would like to fill out a Connect Card ahead of time so we can expect you, click the button below. After filling out the form, when you come visit in person, be sure and stop by the Welcome Tent and let a Dream Teamer know you've filled out a Connect Card online. Don't forget to grab a cup of coffee on your way in as well!
